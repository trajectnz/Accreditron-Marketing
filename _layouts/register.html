Frequently Asked Questions

What's the Pre-Release Trial Programme?
Accreditron is still in Pre-Release, but we want you to get recieve the benefit of the platform as soon as possible. Pre-Release means that Accreditron is not full-feature yet. You can submit your accreditation pre-assessment for review and we will make sure that information makes it to your Assessor.
When can I start using Accreditron?
Soon! Because we are currently running a pre-release trial, we will bring providers on to the system individually. By registering your interest, we will reach out and step you through the process.
Can I tailor Accreditron to my organization?
Accreditron has been co-designed with Social Service Providers an Government Agencies. We are constantly iterating feedback into our product to make sure that it suits your needs. If your organisation would like additional tools or resource on the platform please contact us and let us know!
What government agencies does Accreditron work with?
We are working cross-agency in central government, and our primary agency partner is the Ministry of Social Development. Other government parties to come on board soon are District Health Boards and regional Councils.
